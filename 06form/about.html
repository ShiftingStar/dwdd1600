About Fazbear Entertainment Incorporated

Our Story: From Local Pizza Parlor to Global Entertainment Leader

Fazbear Entertainment began with a simple idea: bring families together through food, fun, and unforgettable characters. The very first Fredbear's Family Diner set the stage, capturing imaginations with friendly animatronics and a welcoming atmosphere. From there, the dream grew into Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, a beloved name that quickly expanded to new locations and new generations of animatronics, each one more advanced and engaging than the last.


Through the years, Fazbear Entertainment has evolved beyond neighborhood restaurants to become a worldwide leader in family entertainment. Our innovations in robotics and interactive experiences have set the standard for the industry, with state-of-the-art animatronics that delight millions across the globe.

The crown jewel of this legacy is the Mega Pizzaplex, a one-of-a-kind destination that brings together dining, attractions, and cutting-edge technology under one roof. It stands as proof of how far Fazbear Entertainment has come—from a single diner to an internationally recognized brand dedicated to fun, innovation, and unforgettable memories.

Meet Our Founders

Willaim Afton - Co-Founder, Lead Engineer

  • Built the Animatronics
  • Built the Springlock Suits
  • Designed Stage Mechanics

William Afton is an engineer, entrepreneur, and co-founder of Fazbear Entertainment Incorporated. Known for his technical brilliance, Afton specialized in pioneering the animatronic construction and early robotics, helping transform small-town entertainment into a national name. His work with springlock suits and interactive characters pushed the boundaries of what family dining experiences could be, blending performance with cutting-edge technology. Afton's legacy lives in every continued evolution of Fazbear Entertainment, particularly felt in every animatronic innovation that delights children and families alike.


Henry Emily - Co-Founder, Creative Director

  • Business Executive
  • Established Fredbear's Family Diner
  • Designed the Animatronics

Henry Emily is the quiet visionary behind Fazbear Entertainment Incorporated, a man of creativity, compassion, and dedication. A gifted designer, Henry built FEI's animatronics to bring joy to children, including—most notably—the Puppet, which he modeled with a father's love after his late daughter, Charlie. While working with Afton keeps Emily from the spotlight, he has remained a steady, watchful presence, ever influencal in all of Fazbear's locations and animatronics, as the business that once started as a small family diner, grew into a project that would create it's own undeniable legacy.


Other Names to Recognize

Michael Afton - Head of Staffing
Michael Afton, Willaim Afton's eldest son, serves as Head of Staffing for Fazbear Entertainment Incorporated, bringing steady leadership and a hands-on approach to building strong, dependable teams across our locations. Having worked his way through several roles within the company, Michael understands the value of dedication, adaptability, and teamwork built from the ground up. He has a reputation for being approachable, hardworking, and willing to step in wherever he's needed most. Michael's focus is always on supporting our staff so that they, in turn, can deliver the best possible experience to our guests. His commitment to people makes him an essential part of the Fazbear family.
Jeremy Fitzgerald - Head of Marketing
Originally hired as night security for Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, an early FEI location, Jeremy Fitzgerald discovered a deep appreciation for what makes Fazbear truly special: the joy, imagination, and sense of wonder our locations bring to families. Inspired by that experience, Jeremy transitioned into marketing, where he now serves as Head of Marketing for Fazbear Entertainment Incorporated. His firsthand knowledge of our operations gives him a unique perspective on what matters most—both to our guests and to our staff. With creativity, enthusiasm, and a genuine love for the brand, Jeremy leads our marketing efforts with the same dedication that first made him stand out as a member of our security team.
Vanessa Ames - Chief of Security
Vanessa is one of Fazbear Entertainment Incorporated's most reliable and versatile team members. Starting as night security at our Utah location, she gained experience and now heads security staff, overseeing multiple locations, bringing consistency, professionalism, and a steady hand to every site she heads. Whether it's ensuring smooth late-night operations, assisting staff, or keeping our guests safe, she has built a reputation for adaptability and dedication. Her cross-location expertise makes her an invaluable part of the Fazbear team.
